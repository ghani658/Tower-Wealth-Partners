Coq au vin

https://www.halfbakedharvest.com/healthier-instant-pot-coq-au-vin/

Fall is one of my favorite times of the year. The produce that you can find at your local farmers market is full of color, hearty root vegetables and, of course, pumpkins. Some of our family’s favorite fall recipes include butternut squash soup, pumpkin donuts, apple pie, and dishes made with low-and-slow braised meats.

This recipe for a healthier Coq Au Vin ticks all of boxes of warm autumn flavors and smells, and it’s especially a great recipe for those of you that have an Instant Pot or a crockpot. Don’t let the fancy name scare you away – this perfect-for-fall recipe is actually quite easy to make (I’m pretty sure my hubby could even pull this one off!). Oh, and don’t forget to pair it with some creamy mashed potatoes, crusty bread, and your favorite red wine.
